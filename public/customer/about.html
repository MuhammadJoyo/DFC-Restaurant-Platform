Our Story

A journey of spices, dedication, and delicious local taste

Since 2016

The Taste of Ghareebabad, Dadu

Dadu Food Corner (DFC) was founded with a single mission: to serve premium, fresh, and hygienic food options that cater to both traditional local cravings and modern fast food loves.

Located in the heart of Ghareebabad, Dadu, we began our journey serving authentic Biryani and mouth-watering BBQ rolls. Recognizing our customers' love for crispiness and cheese, we expanded our kitchen to bring top-tier Crispy Fried Zinger Burgers, Broast, and freshly baked Pizzas that rival global chains in quality but preserve the rich spices preferred by our community.

We pride ourselves on using 100% Halal meats, fresh daily cut vegetables, and imported mozzarella cheese. From a local eatery, DFC has grown into a household name for online deliveries and comfortable family dining in Dadu.
